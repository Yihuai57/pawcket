About Us

Pawcket is a proudly Edmonton-based pet tech startup founded by a passionate team of University of Alberta students. Our diverse backgrounds in computer science, veterinary sciences, and business have come together to create an innovative solution for pet owners.

Our journey began in the UofA dorms where we noticed how challenging it was for student pet owners to manage their furry friends' schedules alongside demanding academic commitments. This sparked the idea for Pawcket - a comprehensive pet management app that simplifies the pet ownership experience.

Our mission is to strengthen the bond between pets and their owners through thoughtful technology. We believe that when pet care is simplified, both pets and owners experience a higher quality of life together.

With Pawcket, we're not just building an app; we're creating a community of caring pet owners in Edmonton and beyond, connected through their love for their animal companions.

Our Vision

We envision a world where pet ownership is seamless, social, and supported by smart technology. Where pet parents never miss an important appointment, where finding pet-friendly spaces is easy, and where pet communities thrive.

Our Values

  • Pet-first thinking: Every feature we develop considers your pet's wellbeing first
  • Local roots: Supporting Edmonton's pet community while growing globally
  • Innovation: Constantly evolving our platform based on pet owner feedback
  • Inclusivity: Creating tools for all types of pets and pet families
